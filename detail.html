Our Gradient

Beef (baked meat), Salad, tomato, onion, vegetable oil, fish sauce, oil, salt, Powder, sugar, garlic, ground pepper.

How to coook?

Beef (baked goods) 3 bites (thin hans)

Salad 1 bite

1 tomato (thinly sliced)

1 onion (thin bean)

1 tablespoon of vegetable oil

1 tablespoons fish sauce

1 teaspoon oil

A little salt

Powder a little bit

1 tablespoon of sugar

4 cloves garlic (shredded)

Half a teaspoon ground pepper

Start Cooking

1/. Place the salad in a bowl of tomato sauce on the salad and place the onion over the next floor (if you do not like the vegetable oil flavor).

2/. Add hot peppers to fry the frying pan and put the meat back in a few turns.

3/. Room to put sugar, oil, salt, flour, rice, cooked meat, pepper meat, and pepper on top of vegetables.
